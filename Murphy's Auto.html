About Us

Murphy's Auto has been satisfying the adventurous side of Hampton Roads for the past 50 years. It started with my grandfather and father back in 1969, and the torch has been passed on to me to maintain a trusted and competent business where men can come to and unleash. No matter whether it is snow, ice, mud, water, hills, rocks or mountains, our rugged line up of trucks and suv's will take you to hell and back.
